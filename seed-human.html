Seed: HUMAN

Seed: HUMAN Seed: HUMAN is a generative photography piece that explores the role Artificial intelligence plays in co-creating artworks with human. To tell a story in a world infiltrated by intelligent machines, through visual language, how much agency do we actually have as human? Are we the photographer, or simply a walking camera? Who is the artist? AI or me? How credible is story teller in this sense? this project consists of a series of images in groups of four. Two machine learning models are used in this project: one for interpreting the content of first image in one sentence, the other for generating a narrative based on that sentence. A human then attempts to visualize the whole narrative by taking 3 subsequent photographs according to the keywords from the generated texts.
