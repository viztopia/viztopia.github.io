About

Yuguang Zhang is a New York-based creative technologist and artist.

An ex-product manager and Certified Business Analysis Professional, he took a leap of faith from his mid-career in 2018, in pursuit of a vision that embraces the co-existence of human and intelligent machines. Using networked devices, sound, mixed reality and Artificial Intelligence, he explores ways to create meaningful experiences in a digital age. He is also actively engaged in traditional West African music and dance performance since 2014.

Yuguang is a current student of the Interactive Telecommunications Program (ITP) at New York University (2020).
